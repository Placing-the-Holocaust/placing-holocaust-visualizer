QUESTION: OK. Would you please tell us your name, and your age, please?

ANSWER: My name is Franz Wohlfahrt. I'm close to seventy years now. And I was born in Kostenberg- Velden , Carinthia [province]. That's in southern Austria .

In that town . It's a beautiful tourist, uh, town , a beautiful lake surrounded by mountains . And 1s visited by many tourists from all over the world.

And there, uh, he had to wait about three months, uh, [to] be transferred from the, uh, Vienna Army jail to Berlin . In the meantime, some of the town[s] people and the former mayor, uh, put up a petition; and, uh, they were signed by most of the people. Even some Nazis sent in letters of recommendations. But this didn't help anything. They beheaded him. And it is really a coincidence that today 1s that very day. The 7th December of 1935 [1939]--fifty years since my father was beheaded.

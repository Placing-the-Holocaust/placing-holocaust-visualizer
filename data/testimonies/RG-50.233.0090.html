ANSWER: My mother’s name is Mushka. The middle name is Zola. And after she married my father she became the last name Menyuk.

QUESTION: And Zola was her maiden name?

ANSWER: Maiden name, yes. She was born approximately 1900 year in village Komarova . That was Poland in this time.

QUESTION: She was born in 1900?

ANSWER: Approximately 1900, yes.

QUESTION: Do you remember what month she was born?

ANSWER: I don’t remember, no.

QUESTION: Did she ever tell you any stories about her childhood in growing up in Komarova ?

ANSWER: No. She got child time not very well. It was a poor family in a village . She was working hard to make the living. And one time, I know only what she was telling, she and the father, the father was in the war, the Russian and German war. She was by herself at home waiting for the father when he will come. I was born after that when the father came from the war. She had a hard time. It was a big family.

QUESTION: It was your father who was in World War 1?

ANSWER: Yes, yes.

QUESTION: And he fought on the Russian side ?

ANSWER: Yes, on the Russian side .

QUESTION: Do you know when your mother got married, what year?

ANSWER: No, it’s hard to tell. Only I can tell you I was born in 1922.

QUESTION: Maybe a few years before you were born?

ANSWER: A few years before I was born, maybe about three. Because she married with the father and he was in the war. Then when he came from the war he was one year, maybe not a full year, he was in Germany , how is it called the QUESTION: Oh, was he a prisoner of war?

ANSWER: Prisoner of war, yes, a war prisoner in Germany . And then after he came I was born, after a year.

QUESTION: Can you tell me a little bit about your mother’s parents, your grandparents? Did you know them very well?

ANSWER: Yes. I remember only the grandfather. When I was study in Sarna and I was living by my aunt, that’s my mother’s sister’s family and one time we went to the uncle want to come to us in the village to Komarova , and he said we will come in a place where my grandfather lives. We stayed maybe one night and then we came to Komarova to my home with my uncle from Sarna . That’s what I remember my grandfather. My grandmother, I don’t remember at all.

ANSWER: He was living in this time, he was living in Lapecica . That’s a place close to Rovna , Sarna . What he was doing, I don’t remember. I saw him only when he was sick.

Most of them a couple of grades and that’s it. People go to work, some cook, Many different business. That’s what I think she didn’t get a high education. The Germans came and making all the bad start.

A couple, the Germans begin to come to us in 10 days.
